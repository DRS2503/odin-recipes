Spaghetti

Home

Description

The classic Italian inspired recipe featuring a pasta tossed in a rich tomato saucee with garlic, olive oil, and fresh herbs

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti: 200g
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Garlic cloves: 3
  • Crushed tomatos: 1 can
  • Salt: to taste
  • Black pepper: .5 tsp
  • Chili flakes: .5 tsp
  • Italian seasoning: 1 tsp
  • Parmesan chese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot to a boil
  2. Add spaghetti and cook according to package
  3. Reserve .5 cup of pasta water then drain the rest
  4. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat
  5. Add the chopped garlic and saute for 1-2 minutes
  6. add the crushed tomatoes, salt, pepper, chili flakes, and italian seasoning
  7. Stir well and simmer for 10-15 minutes, uncovered, stiring occasionally
  8. Add the cooked spaghetti directly into the sauce
  9. Toss to coat, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce
  10. Taste and adjust seasoning
  11. plate the spaghetti, and top with grated parmesan
