Taco Stew

Home

Describe the recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast: 1lbs
  • Taco Seasoning: 1 packet
  • Black beans: 1 can
  • Chillie beans: 2 cans
  • Tomato paste: 1 8oz can
  • Diced tomatos: 2 cans
  • Corn: 1 can

Steps

  1. Get slow cooker out
  2. Open corn and drain waiter with striner
  3. Add the corn to the slow cooker
  4. Open the rest of the cans and add them to the slow cooker (they do not need to be drained)
  5. Add taco seasoning
  6. Add chicken breast
  7. Set slow cooker to hand and set 3 hour timer
  8. Enjoy
