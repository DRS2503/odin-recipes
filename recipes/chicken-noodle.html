Chicken Noodle

Home

This is a warm and comforting simple dish known for a long time.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil: 1 tbsp
  • Medium Onion: 1
  • Garlic Cloves: 3
  • carrots: 3
  • Celery: 3
  • Chicken Broth: 8 cups
  • Cooked Chicken: 2 cups
  • Noodles: 2 cups
  • Dried Parsley: 1 tsp
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop vegtables
  2. In a large bot head olive oil over medium heat
  3. Add vegtables and cook for 5-7 minutes
  4. Add garlic and cook for a 1 more minute
  5. Pour chicken broth
  6. Add parsley, salt and pepper
  7. Bring to a gentle boil
  8. Add noodles to the boiling broth
  9. Cook according to package instructions
  10. Add in the coocked shredded chicken
  11. simmer for 2-3 minutes
  12. Add more salt add pepper if needed
